Ball Bounce

A game where you try to stop the ball from hitting the bottom with a paddel. The twist is that the ball is going a little bit faster each time it bounces on the paddle.
Written in JS with the library p5.js You can play the game here.

Paddle Battle

A similar game as the previouse one. This is a 2-player game with the goal to keep the ball at your side of the board. Each player has a timer ticking when the ball is on their side and the first player to hit the score limit wins.
Written in JS with the library p5.js You can play the game here.
